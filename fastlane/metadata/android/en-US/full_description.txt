Easily track your journey towards freedom with Quitter.

See how long it's been since you've quit one of many addictions.
Be notified (at a frequency of your choice) when you reach certain milestones.
Get educated on the benefits of quitting!

Our app is completely Free and Open Source. That means no ads, and no tracking, ever! 
If you notice any bugs or want to suggest features, please feel free to visit us at https://github.com/brandonp2412/Quitter/issues
